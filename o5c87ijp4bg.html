Sensitivity analysis

To assess the sensitivity of our analysis to outlier data points in the OMICs features, the analysis was run multiple times with caps on the OMICs features to remove outliers above or below specified percentiles (specifically, 5%, 10% and 20%).

Multi-OMIC integrative analysis

The GLS model identified four sets of biomarkers for each of the OMIC levels that have significant associations with the experimentally-induced change in TRAP exposure. OMICs integrative analysis was then performed on these OMIC sets, which included network analysis and sparse partial least squares (sPLS) regression, in order to discover any potential biological significance.
