The proportion of variance in multivariate Y explained by the retained X component is low, with most of the explored OMIC pairs showing less than 5% explanatory performance. The highest OMIC pair being metabolite (X) and mRNA (Y), in which the retained component selected 10/214 metabolites to explain 10.4% of the variation in Y, which is one mRNA.
