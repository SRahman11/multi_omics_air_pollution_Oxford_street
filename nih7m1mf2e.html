Discussion

Our study entailed a two step strategy to explore the effects of short-term exposure to air pollution at a multi-OMIC level. The first step was to run an univariate analysis to explore the relationships between each short term exposure to the different- OMIC levels separately for 50 participants.
Results from our univariate analysis show that,  one mRNA, six miRNAs, 148 metabolites and 2 adductomics were found to be significantly associated with TRAP exposure. For the single mRNA, three metabolites and one adductomic  were significantly associated with more than one TRAP.   Further analysis were conducted in Results reported by our analysis generally support results found in published literature on the topic.
TRAP-associated metabolites were analysed using the mummichog algorithm at https://www.metaboanalyst.ca to predict functional activity. Five metabolomic pathways were identified as having significant levels of metabolomic modification in our results.
