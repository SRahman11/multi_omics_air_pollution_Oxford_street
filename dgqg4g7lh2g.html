Adductomics

Adductomics is a more recent addition to the OMICs field than metabolomics or transcriptomics, and there are comparatively few analytical analytical tools and sparser literature. However, one of the adductomics identified in our results as being positively mediated by TRAP exposure, d_107.1, has also been reported in the literature as being positively mediated by smoking.\cite{Preston_2017} This suggests a potential common biological pathway towards DNA damage and mutation between TRAP exposure and smoking.
