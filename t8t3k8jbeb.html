OMICs measurement

For each participant and session, three blood serum samples were collected at two hours before the walk, two hours after, and 24 hours after, on which multi-omics measurements were taken, whiincluded metabolomics, transcriptomics (mRNA and miRNA), and adductomics. Protocols for the metabolomic and miRNA measurements can be found in  earlier  articles by Veldhoven and Krauskopf  respectively (Veldhoven, 2019) (Krauskopf, 2018). After removal and imputation of missing data, there remained full measurements for: 418 miRNAs; 30,981 mRNAs; 5,844 metabolites and 92 adductomics. 

Covariates

For each participant, measurements were taken for height, weight, BMI, systolic blood pressure (SBP), diasystolic blood pressure (DBP) and recent caffeine intake.

Statistical analysis

OMICs Profiling
