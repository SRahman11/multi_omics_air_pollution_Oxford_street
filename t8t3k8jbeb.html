OMICs measurement

For each participant and session, blood serum samples were collected at two hours before the walk, two hours after, and 24 hours after, from which multi-omics measurements were taken, including metabolomics, transcriptomics (mRNA and miRNA), and adductomics. After removal and imputation of missing data, there remained full measurements for: 418 miRNAs; 30,981 mRNAs; 5,844 metabolites and 92 adductomics.

Covariates

For each participant, measurements were taken for height, weight, BMI, systolic blood pressure (SBP), disystolic blood pressure (DBP) and recent caffeine intake.

Statistical analysis

After removing observations for any participants who were current smokers or who had incomplete measurements, dta 50 complete observations were analysed.
