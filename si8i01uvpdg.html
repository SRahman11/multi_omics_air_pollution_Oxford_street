Associations between exposure and OMIC response were analysed using generalised least squares (GLS) regression models, incorporating a general unstructured variance-covariance matrix, which the transformation matrix is derived from, to account for heteroscedasticity and autocorrelation in the data arising from an experimental design in which three repeated measurements were taken in two locations for each individual.
