Health status

Analysis of the OMICs data reveals some structure relating to health status (healthy/CPOD/IHD), at each of the measured timepoints (both pre- and post-exposure), especially at the metabolomic level. Figure X shows each the outcome of a principal component analysis of the metabolomic data: the first two principal components for each observation are plotted at the three time points (before exposure, after exposure, and 24 hours after exposure).
